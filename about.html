HARIS FARM STEAD

Farmers empowerment and Fund Farm Operations

The HARIS FARM STEAD was borne out of our desire as an organization to add value to our community; increase the content of our nation’s food basket with cutting edge technology and infrastructure; serve as a home for farming, manufacturing and production; and ensure food security. No matter how outstanding the industrial and manufacturing sectors of any nation are, its economy will collapse if it’s agricultural sector is underperforming. Upon realizing these, we came together to create solutions to many agricultural problems in one single platform.

The factors that make the Haris Farm Stead unique are; digitalized agriculture, farm proximity to major markets, availability and affordability of farm land, proper agri-education for participants, and the grand master-plan of the stead which consists of infrastructural development and 24/7 modern electrification among others. Since the inception of the HARIS FARM STEAD, we have

  • Farmers empowered
  • Farms sponsored
  • Acres cultivated
  • Farm sponsors and community members
